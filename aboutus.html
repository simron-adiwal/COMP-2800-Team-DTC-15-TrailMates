About Us


Jason, Joseph, Simron, and Wani
were united by their common love for the great outdoors, hiking (and the whims of their CST instructors) in the wild, wild west of BCIT's downtown campus.

They thought that exploring the scenic trails of BC by yourself is great, hiking with others is even better. However, a common problem was that it was hard to schedule hikes with friends.

An app seemed like the perfect tool to organize hikes with your friends or find new people to hike with. However, when they tried to find such an app, the closest thing that they could find was hiking reviews and directions apps.

Thus, they took it upon themselves to create the first ever social media platform for hiking enthusiasts:


TrailMates
