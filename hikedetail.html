Garibaldi Lake

View of Garibaldi Lake
Garibaldi Map
Previous Next

Location: Squamish-Lillooet Regional, BC

Length: 18.2km

Difficulty: 3/5

Conditions/Hazards:

  • Beware of bears from May to October
  • Snow storms from November to February

Description: City, ocean, and mountain views, combined with window shopping and understanding the history of the city.


Accessibility: There are many parking spaces in the paved parking lots at the northeast end of the trail at the intersection of Carall and Powell, but it is unclear if any of them have designated accessible spaces or striped access aisles. The trail surface is paved (sidewalk, brick, or asphalt) and typically 1 - 2 meters wide.
